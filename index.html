About Us:

Whether you want to swim laps, join an AquaFIT class, enrol the kids in swimming lessons or simply cool off, the pool is the place to be! This is a great find for families. This pool is actually two facilities: a large, heated L-shaped pool with a diving section and a smaller teaching pool with a constant depth of 3.5 feet. The spacious pool deck is equipped with lounge chairs and tables, and toys such as flutterboards and pool noodles. An outstanding team of aquatics professionals lead programs and oversee the operations. All of this makes our pool the best place in Ottawa to spend any day.
